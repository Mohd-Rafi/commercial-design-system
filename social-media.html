Social Media

A "Social Media Feed" in web UI design refers to a section or component within a website or application that displays a chronological stream of content from various social media platforms. This content can include text posts, images, videos, links, and other types of media shared by users or accounts that a person follows. Social media feeds are commonly used to aggregate and present updates and posts from different sources in a unified and easily digestible format.
